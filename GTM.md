# Go-To-Market Strategy

**Exact Target User:**
VP of Engineering or fractional CTOs at Series A/B startups (50-200 employees). They are scaling rapidly, have lost track of shadow IT, and are feeling board pressure to improve burn multiples without sacrificing developer velocity.

**What they Google/scroll:**
They search for "reduce AWS bill," "Copilot enterprise vs business," "is Claude Team worth it," or they scroll Hacker News threads complaining about SaaS bloat and OpenAI API costs.

**Where they hang out online:**
- Secret Slack communities (e.g., Rands Leadership Slack, specifically the `#cto` or `#engineering-managers` channels).
- YC Founder Slack (if backed by Y Combinator).
- Subreddits: `r/SaaS`, `r/ExperiencedDevs`.

**First 100 users in 30 days ($0 budget):**
1. **The "Tear-down" Cold Email:** Find 50 Series A CTOs recently announced on TechCrunch. Send a cold email: "I noticed you just scaled to 100 people. Assuming 80 engineers, you are likely overspending ~$14k/yr on GitHub Copilot Enterprise alone. Run your exact numbers here: [Link]."
2. **Open Source the Logic:** Package the `auditEngine.ts` logic into an open-source CLI tool called `ai-spend-cli`. Post it to Hacker News ("Show HN: We built a CLI to audit shadow AI spend"). The CLI prompts users to check the web UI for deeper visualization and Credex credits.

**The Unfair Distribution Channel:**
Credex's existing network of over-forecasted companies. If Company A is selling off excess Copilot seats through Credex, we can ask Company A's investors (VCs) to share our Audit Tool across their *entire portfolio* to help their other startups save money—driving massive B2B virality through trusted investor intros.

**Week-1 Traction:**
- 250 Unique Visitors (driven by the HN launch and Slack seeding).
- 40 Audits Completed (16% conversion rate).
- 5 Qualified Leads (>$500/mo savings identified) booking a Credex consultation.
